      How to Play: SPOKES Goal Tracker

      The 5 Levels

      Progress through five goal-setting levels. Each level unlocks a new prompt card as you scroll.

      1. Level 1 - BHAG: Set your Big, Hairy, Audacious Goal. Available from the start.
      2. Level 2 - Monthly: Define a monthly goal. Unlocks when you lock in your BHAG.
      3. Level 3 - Weekly: Plan weekly actions. Unlocks after Monthly + 5 daily check-ins.
      4. Level 4 - Daily: Set daily priorities. Unlocks after Weekly + 1 weekly review.
      5. Level 5 - Tasks: Create action tasks. Unlocks after completing your Daily goal.

      Earning XP

      Everything you do earns Experience Points. Accumulate XP to show your engagement.

      Streak Bonuses

      Check in on consecutive days to earn one-time streak milestone bonuses.

      • 3-day streak: +25 XP
      • 7-day streak: +75 XP
      • 14-day streak: +150 XP
      • 30-day streak: +300 XP

      Mission Control

      As you level up, new Mission Control sections unlock:

      • Level 1-2: Daily Momentum + Archive
      • Level 3: SMART Goals, Growth Prompts, Plan Generation
      • Level 4: Kanban board editing
      • Level 5: Vision Board + full access

      Completion Certificate

      To unlock the certificate, reach Level 5, complete all 5 core prompts, have at least one SMART-ready goal (5/5 score), and one completed daily entry.
